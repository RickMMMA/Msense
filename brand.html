M&SENSE (SUZHOU) ARTS & CRAFTS CO., LTD. established in 2009, 
has always been committed to doing stylish home 
fragrance products. As a professional manufacturer
 M&SENSE has cooperated with many
 international brands for years and established good reputation and 
accumulated abundant experience
 in the field of design, flavoring and production.  

With the high quality and good reputation M&Sense
 has successfully entered into the market of UK, France, Germany,
 Norway, Sweden, Denmark, lreland, the United States, Japan, 
the United Arab Emirates. Canada  and Australia. 
After many years of development M&SENSE has made remarkable
 achievements in aromatic industry in various countries.  

In year of 2016 M&SENSE decided to bring the high-quality
 aromatic products back to home market, setting up a subsidiary
 company - Sensus (Suzhou) Industry Co., Ltd. After a year of hard work, 
M&SENSE has established distribution channels in Beijing, 
Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen. Hangzhou, Nanjing and other cities, 
and tapped into online sales in 2017.
